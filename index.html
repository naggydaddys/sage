About

SAGE is a Coffeehouse and Tea Garden that has been in historic downtown Pottsville, PA for over 10 years. We are proud of the fact that we have never raised the price of our coffee since the day we opened!

SAGE is also available for catering your special event and hosts Children's Tea Parties on the weekend!

Art

SAGE is proud to support the local artist community. Since opening, the walls have always featured local art and multiple 'Artist Openings' are held thru the year showcasing new art on display and a chance to meet the artist. See our contact page for more info!

Dress-Up Tea Parties

Celebrate a Birthday, Special Occasion, Good Report Card or just a FUN Day!

Each child receives finger sandwiches, snacks, celery & carrot sticks, cake, and beverages!

We provide all of the dress-up clothing including dresses, shoes, jewelry, hats and more. We also paint fingernails!

Parties are 2 hours long and cost $12.00 per child. Min of 10 children. Dates & times are filling quickly, Reserve yours today!
